Background

Famous Play by William Shakespeare
Prince Hamlet is visited by the ghost of his father the king.
The king is murdered by his brother, who marries the queen and takes the throne
Hamlet plots to take revenge on the king.

Themes

Is it better to live in pain or die, since we don’t know what happens after death?
Subjectivism - “there is nothing good or bad, but thinking makes it so”
Religion(Christianity)
Hamlet’s father dies without last rites and is in purgatory
Hamlet has to decide whether to avenge his father or leave it to God


Rosencrantz and Guildenstern are Dead

Absurdist, existentialist tragicomedy 
Follows the activities of Rosencrantz and Guildenstern, who are side characters in the original Hamlet.
They struggle against their fate, not knowing they are scripted to die.
Explores ideas of art versus reality, the struggle against destiny, and whether there is meaning in life and death
